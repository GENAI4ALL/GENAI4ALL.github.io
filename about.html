Who Are We?

Lead Faculty

Wei Chen, Associate Professor, University of Connecticut

Wei Chen is an Associate Professor of Operations and Information Management (OPIM) at the School of Business, University of Connecticut. He is also the academic director of the UConn Digital Frontiers Initiative, which aims to facilitate collaborations between industry and academia, with a current emphasis on the business applications of generative AI.

Wei's current research focuses on the design and governance of generative AI. His work on Platforms, Crowds, and Financial Technologies (FinTech) has been published in leading academic journals, such as Management Science, Information Systems Research, and MIS Quarterly. Wei teaches Generative AI for Business in the graduate programs at UConn Business School, and frequently speaks to industry partners and conferences on the topic.
