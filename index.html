Welcome to GenAI4All. We're a community of business professors passionate about making Generative AI (GenAI) accessible to everyone. We’ve taught multipleGenAI courses to business school students and learned three key lessons:

  • Students lack engaging, structured resources on Generative AI.
  • Understanding GenAI doesn't require advanced coding skills.
  • Using GenAI effectively doesn’t have to be expensive.

This website is our mission in action: to make GenAI accessible, practical, and affordable for everyone. Explore the course materials on our course page, and sign up below to stay updated with the latest news!
