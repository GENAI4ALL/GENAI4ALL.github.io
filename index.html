Make Generative AI Accessible

Welcome to GenAI4All. We're two business professors passionate about making Generative AI (GenAI) accessible to everyone. We have taught GenAI courses to undergraduate and master students at University of Connecticut and University of Cincinnati. Along our teaching journey, we discovered three crucial truths:

  • Students lack access to systematic, engaging resources on Generative AI.
  • You don’t need to be a coding wizard to grasp GenAI concepts and applications.
  • Harnessing the power of GenAI doesn’t have to cost a fortune.

This website is our mission in action: to break down barriers and make Generative AI accessible, practical, and affordable for everyone. Whether you're a student, a professional, or simply curious, you're in the right place to explore, learn, and innovate.
