About This Course

This course is designed for business students. Drawing from our experience teaching in-person, online, and hybrid formats in graduate programs at the University of Connecticut and the University of Cincinnati, we’ve learned three key lessons:

  • Students lack engaging, structured resources on Generative AI.
  • Understanding GenAI doesn't require advanced coding skills.
  • Using GenAI effectively doesn’t have to be expensive.

We’re excited to share our course content below. Each module includes topics, curated reading materials, and episodes from our course to provide a comprehensive learning experience. Don’t just take our word for it—explore what our students have to say through their testimonials.
