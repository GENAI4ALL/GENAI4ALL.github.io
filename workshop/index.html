GenAI Workshop

Date: December 18-20, 2025

Instructor: Wei Chen
Academic Director, Digital Frontiers Initiative
Associate Professor, Operations and Information Management

This executive workshop explores how generative AI is reshaping the future of business, from the fundamentals of prompt engineering to the emerging role of autonomous agents. We will begin with a practical introduction to generative AI (GenAI), focusing on large language models (LLMs) and their business applications. The sessions will examine how organizations can adapt and deploy these models through strategies like prompt engineering, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), and agentic systems to meet domain-specific challenges. The curriculum balances conceptual frameworks with hands-on exercises, enabling participants to apply generative AI to real-world business challenges effectively.
