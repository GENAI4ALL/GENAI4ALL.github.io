1.1 Overview of Generative AI

What is Generative AI?

Generative Artificial Intelligence represents a paradigm shift in how we think about and interact with technology. Unlike traditional AI systems that are designed to recognize patterns or make predictions, generative AI creates new content‚Äîwhether that's text, images, code, or other forms of data.

At its core, generative AI learns from vast amounts of existing data to understand patterns, structures, and relationships, then uses this understanding to generate novel outputs that didn't exist in its training data. This capability has profound implications for business, as it enables automation of creative and analytical tasks that were previously thought to require human intelligence.

What's Different about Generative AI?

The distinction between generative AI and previous AI technologies is fundamental. Traditional AI systems were primarily discriminative‚Äîthey could classify, predict, or optimize based on existing data. Generative AI, however, is creative in nature. It doesn't just analyze what exists; it creates what could be.

This shift from analysis to creation opens up entirely new possibilities for business applications...
