How to join us?

Dear developers and experts in the field of artificial intelligence,

We are conducting a platform project on GitHub that focuses on artificial intelligence education and research. The project aims to build an open and collaborative environment to promote the sharing of artificial intelligence knowledge and the development of technology. We sincerely invite you to participate in this project, whether you are a senior researcher, educator, or a beginner passionate about artificial intelligence.

We look forward to your professional knowledge and innovative thinking bringing valuable contributions to this project. Through collaborative efforts, we can drive the advancement of artificial intelligence technology and provide support to the global academic and industrial communities. Please visit our GitHub repository to view project details and participate in various stages such as code development, document writing, and problem solving.

We believe that through collective wisdom and effort, we can achieve a more intelligent and interconnected future. Looking forward to meeting you on GitHub and witnessing every step of this journey together.
